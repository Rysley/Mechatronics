    MECHATRONICS

    Mechatronics (the word comes from the combination of the terms mechanics and electronics) is a field of science that integrates mechanics, electronics, automation, computer science, and other technical and non-technical areas. It integrates some of the more difficult sciences, which is why it is becoming more popular in the labor market.

    Thanks to its reliable features and simplicity of use, mechatronics has significantly relieved humans of many difficult tasks

    Mechatronic is something that we are facing in everyday life, thanks to it was created among other:

    • robots, for example robot ,,da Vinci” – used in medicine

    • automatically opening doors

    • automatic barrier (at the entrance to the parking, at rail crossings)

    • traffic lights

    PARTNERS


    CVT

    KARATZIOS-FERFELIS ANTONIOS-KARATZIOU-FERFELI KYRIKI O.E CENTER FOR VOCATIONAL TRAINING is a partner that for many years has been a host organization for EU mobility programmes, especially in the VET sector. The institution is based in Larissa, one of the largest cities in Greece, in which the mechatronics and information technology industry has been developing for many years



    In its educational activity:

    • specializes in organizing internships for students of technical and vocational schools and training trips with a special focus on IT.

    • cooperates with many Greek companies in the Olympic Riviera and Larissa region.

    • cooperates with IT companies, including Wizcom from Larissa as well as smaller entities, including bakeries (Sdougas), and smaller service offices.

    • has been organizing internships abroad for students and graduates of vocational schools since 2010. During this time, more than 20 mobilities were organized, in which more than 500 participants took part in internships and vocational internships realized in the field of mechanics, mechatronics and IT.

    • organizes workshops and conferences for schools, during which it jointly discusses planned projects and is a so-called multiplier of contacts between schools.

    • it also organizes preparatory visits for schools which intend to undertake international cooperation and organize internships and vocational internships abroad for their students and graduates.

    • Many years of experience in the industry results in the wider competences of the company’s employees in the field of project menagment, which allows to ensure the safety, comfort of participants, but also the stability of the cooperation, which is based on the rules defined at the beginning and accepted by the partners.

    EDUNET

    Edunet Europe, n.o. was founded in 2018. It’s team has a huge experience in the field of education, training and organization of various events. As a result of long-term experience and pleasant interpersonal relationships they work precisely, accurately and effectively to ensure the satisfaction of the groups. We take into consideration the priorities that are specified by the EU, the general objectives of the ERASMUS+ program, the goals and the expected learning results/outcomes of the foreign schools in all cases. Since its establishment our organisation has been in active contact with secondary vocational institutes. With the program ERASMUS+ each year we organize a workshop for them as an opportunity to share their experiences, to brainstorm together and to develop interesting partnerships. We believe that secondary education must keep pace with the requirements of the modern age, in order to increase the chances of vocational training students entering the labor market and our aim is to enable them the gain all the skills and experiences they might need.We are in direct contact with schools, we support their application and sending process as a contractual partner, making sure our knowledge and experience will ensure a quality mobility for the students and teachers.

    They are constantly looking for new challenges and collaborations that, in addition to young people and those working in education, are actively responding to the challenges of the civil sphere. We try to find solutions to the problems of others that may cause difficulty for people of different ages and views. Through the activities of those working in education and training, opportunities and competencies are developed for an effective solution. Edunet supports local communities by motivating and promoting an active attitude, we educate and help others to acquire knowledge and skills, to activate them professionally, to cooperate with other countries, to implement and disseminate the idea of volunteering, especially for young people.

    With the organisation they mainly function as a coordinating and sending organisation for VET mobilities and Visegrad Found and other local funds. Locally the organization works with volunteers, youth and adults organising them different social activities and educational programmes. The organisation’s members are experts, who ensure quality and modern education for students of various professions and to enable them to gain work experience which will open up new opportunities on the labor market domestic and foreign as well. The aim of the organization is to build an international network of contacts, which will help to improve the quality of education giving it an international character.

    Project No. 2020-1-PL01-KA202-082263 „European quality of education - new technologies as a chance for the success of young Europeans” is funded by the European Union among Erasmus+ program.
